Hi, I am Sujal Ramesh.

Enthusiastic, Innovative & Passionate about Games

I'm an Indian programmer and game designer who has been creating games since completing 12th grade. My passion for video games, which I played extensively in my youth, evolved into a desire to create my own. Currently, I'm pursuing a B.Sc. in game development.
